Single Player Two Player Multiplayer Top Players

Tic-tac-toe or Xs and Os is paper and pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.
